About the species
🐟

Conservation Status

Cownose rays (Rhinoptera bonasus) are Near Threatened due to bycatch and unregulated recreational fishing pressure along the Atlantic coast.

🌊

Seasonal Migration

They migrate in large aggregations — sometimes thousands — northward in spring and return south in fall, often entering the Chesapeake Bay.

📡

Telemetry Research

Tagged individuals provide depth, temperature, and track data that refines our understanding of habitat use and bycatch risk windows.

📸

Photo Documentation

Submitted photos help researchers verify sightings, assess aggregation size, and document unusual behaviors or injuries in the field.
